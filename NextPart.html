The Nightmare Before Christmas sequel is written, expecting an adaptation

Disney revealed that the novel 'The Nightmare Before Christmas' about the sequel to the original film of the same name produced in 1993 is about to be released in the near future. This is one of Disney's animated "masterpieces" that is loved by many audiences and fans.

Author Shea Ernshaw shared: "The sequel will be written from Sally's point of view. It is a beautiful untold love story between Sally and Jack. What's more special when we see Sally will be the bearer of the title of Pumpkin Queen of Halloween Town. The Nightmare Before Christmas hopes to give fans a long-awaited "spiritual potion" about Sally, Jack and all the familiar inhabitants of the town. It will also introduce a new and somewhat ferocious cast of characters, which I hope readers will enjoy.”

Shea Ernshaw added: “In this book, I really wanted to discover more about Sally. Not merely about who she is, and the love between her and Jack, but will learn more about how Sally's past has influenced her present. As we will know, Sally is a kind girl who is willing to take risks to save the people she loves. Sally is one of the most engaging and diverse characters I've ever written and it's an honor to tell her story."
