Ideas

Ideas for The Nightmare Before Christmas

Originated from a poem by Tim Burton written in 1982, when he was an animator at Walt Disney Animation Studios. With the success of Vincent that same year, Walt Disney Studios began to consider developing The Nightmare Before Christmas into a short film or a 30-minute TV show.

Over the years, Burton's thoughts gradually returned to this project, and in 1990, he decided to work with Disney to develop this film. Film production began in July 1991 in San Francisco. Disney released the film under their Touchstone Pictures label, saying the film was "too dark and scary for young children".
